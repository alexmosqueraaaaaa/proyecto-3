THE LION

The Lion (Panthera leo) is a carnivorous mammal of the felid family and one of five species in the genus Panthera. Wild lions live in increasingly scattered and fragmented populations in sub-Saharan Africa and a small area of northwestern India , having disappeared from the rest of South Asia, West Asia, North Africa and the Balkan Peninsula in historical times. Until the end of the Pleistocene, about ten thousand years ago, of the large land mammals, the lion was the most widespread after humans. Its distribution covered most of Africa, much of Eurasia, from western Europe to India, and in the Americas, from the Yukon River to southern Mexico.
